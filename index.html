In a town like Twin Peaks, nobody is innocent...

Let us go to the past, to before Laura Palmer's murder, and explore the darkness and horror lurking right underneath the surface of the peaceful small town of Twin Peaks. Director David Lynch continues the twisted saga of Twin Peaks. Starring Sheryl Lee as Laura Palmer. Featuring David Bowie, Ray Wise and Kyle McLachlan.

Coming out in May 1992
